IEEE SMARTCOMP 2022 [Under Construction]

IEEE International Conference on Smart Computing (SMARTCOMP) is the premier IEEE conference on smart computing. Smart computing is based on the synergistic combination of advances in Sensor-based technologies, the Internet of Things (IoT), Cyber-Physical Systems, Edge cloud computing, Big Data analytics, Machine Learning, Cognitive Computing, and Artificial Intelligence.
SMARTCOMP 2022 is the 8th edition of the conference and will be held in Aalto University, Espoo, Finland, hosted by Aalto University School of Electrical Engineering. SMARTCOMP 2022 will include smart computing innovations pertaining to pervasive/ubiquitous computing, edge cloud computing, sensor networks, IoT, big data analytics, security and privacy, social computing, cognitive computing, cyber-physical systems and their application and validation within smart computing environments. This includes applications such as smart buildings, smart cities, smart grids, precision agriculture and other innovations contributing to smart living.
Extended versions of selected high quality SMARTCOMP 2022 papers will be considered for publication in Pervasive and Mobile Computing Journal.
