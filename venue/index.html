Aalto University

SMARTCOMP 2022 is the 8th edition of the conference and will be held in Aalto University, Espoo, Finland, hosted by Aalto University School of Electrical Engineering. Aalto University is a public research university located in Espoo, Finland, within the Greater Helsinki metropolitan area. It was established in 2010 as a merger of three major Finnish universities: the Helsinki University of Technology, the Helsinki School of Economics and the University of Art and Design Helsinki. The close collaboration between the scientific, business and arts communities is intended to foster multi-disciplinary education and research.
